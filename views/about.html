HOW IT WORKS

This application displays the location of all of the bell towers in Venice. The map also displays which bell towers we have recordings of its bells ringing (yellow), bell towers we have panoramic views from (orange), bell towers that we have both recordings and views (red) and bell towers that have neither piece of data (white).

Zoom

Click the PLUS/MINUS button in the top left to zoom in or out on a bell tower.

More info

Click on a bell tower to get some basic information about the bell tower, to play the audio recording if it exists, to get to its Venipedia page and to get more information about the bell tower..

Choose CANAL SEGMENTS to see a map with selectable segment, CANALS to see a map with selectable canals, and BRIDGES to see a map with selectable bridges.
